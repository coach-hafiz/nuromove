About

NuroMove specializes in providing fitness coaching services for children with Autism Spectrum Disorder. Our approach is tailored to each child's needs, focusing on enhancing their physical wellness and overall development. With a team of dedicated professionals, we are committed to making a positive impact on every child's life.
